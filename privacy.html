Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

SARF collects limited information when you download and use it, including your device's IP address, pages visited within the Application, time spent on the Application, and your mobile operating system. This information is used solely to improve the Application and provide the service.

Artificial Intelligence

SARF uses AI technologies powered by OpenAI to process your financial questions and provide responses. Your messages are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing in accordance with OpenAI's privacy policy. SARF does not store your AI conversation history on any external server — all data remains on your device.

Personally Identifiable Information

SARF may collect your name as entered in your profile. This information is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to the Service Provider's servers.

Third Party Access

The Application uses OpenAI's API for AI features. Only the content of your AI queries is shared with OpenAI for processing. No financial data, transaction history, or personal profile information is sold or shared with any third party for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

All financial data you enter is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time from Settings → Clear All Data. If you uninstall the Application, all locally stored data is removed from your device.

Children

SARF is not intended for children under 13 years of age. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

The Service Provider implements appropriate technical safeguards to protect your information. Your API key is stored in obfuscated form on your device. Financial data never leaves your device except for AI query processing via OpenAI.

Your Rights
